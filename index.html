What It Entails

Seven standards. Seventy-seven days.

📖

Read Scripture

Read 5–8 Bible chapters every day.

🙏

Pray Twice

Begin and end the day in prayer.

🎧

Worship Only

Limit music with lyrics to worship music.

💪

Train Daily

Complete two intentional workouts.

🚶

Walk

Take an intentional walk during the day.

🤝

Check In

Stay accountable with others in the challenge.
