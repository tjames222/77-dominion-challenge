About the Challenge

This is a season of intentional formation.

The 77-Day Dominion Challenge is not a casual fitness streak or another self-improvement gimmick with better lighting. It is a focused commitment to bring your spiritual life, mind, and body under faithful stewardship before God.

Each day includes seven standards. They are simple enough to remember, hard enough to require discipline, and meaningful enough to shape the person you are becoming.

What It Entails

Seven standards. Seventy-seven days.

📖

Read Scripture

Read 5–8 Bible chapters every day.

🙏

Pray Twice

Begin and end the day in prayer.

🎧

Worship Only

Limit music with lyrics to worship music.

💪

Train Daily

Complete two intentional workouts.

🚶

Walk

Take an intentional walk during the day.

🤝

Check In

Stay accountable with others in the challenge.

The Science

Why 77 days?

🧠Habit formation takes time

Research suggests habits often take longer than the famous 21-day myth. Many behaviors average around 66 days to become more automatic.

🔁Repetition builds identity

Doing the same faithful actions daily reduces negotiation and trains your brain to expect the pattern.

📈Visible progress helps

Tracking your standards turns discipline into something concrete. Progress you can see is easier to continue.

Biblical Reasoning

Dominion means stewardship.

🏃Discipline the body

1 Corinthians 9:27 gives the challenge its theme: purposeful self-control in service of a higher calling.

🕊️Walk by the Spirit

Galatians 5:22–23 names self-control as fruit of the Spirit, not a personality trait reserved for impressive people.

📖Renew the mind

Romans 12:2 points to transformation through renewal. Scripture, prayer, and worship reshape attention.
